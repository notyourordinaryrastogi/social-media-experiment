Study Information & Consent

You are invited to participate in a research study on social media interactions by Brain and Behaviour Lab.

As a part of the study, you will be asked to log in to a social media platform called 'snap.everyday' on which people upload their everyday pictures or pictures of random things they find interesting. You will view a series of those posts and comments and rate your perception of them. This study will take approximately 20–25 minutes. This task would require your utmost attention and honesty.

Your responses will be anonymous and used only for academic research purposes. You may withdraw at any time before submission.

Some content may involve social interactions that you may find uncomfortable.
